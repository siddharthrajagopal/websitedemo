About The Book

At Data as the Fourth Pillar, we explore the profound influence of data in our lives through the lens of storytelling. This book, authored by two insightful writers, delves into how data shapes our understanding of the world around us.

With a blend of engaging anecdotes and rigorous analysis, we aim to enlighten readers about the pivotal role data plays in various aspects of society.
