About The Book

At Data as the Fourth Pillar, we explore the profound influence of data in our lives through the lens of storytelling. This book, authored by two insightful writers, delves into how data shapes our understanding of the world around us.

With a blend of engaging anecdotes and rigorous analysis, we aim to enlighten readers about the pivotal role data plays in various aspects of society.

Key Themes Explored

Data Intensity

Explore the foundations of data science and its real-world applications in decision-making.

Data Operating Model

Discover how to build and maintain effective data operations in your organization.

Business Value KPIs

Learn to measure and track the impact of data initiatives on business outcomes.

Data Maturity Journey

Navigate the path to data maturity with practical insights and strategies.

Audi Case Study

Explore how Audi transformed their business through data-driven decision making and AI implementation.
